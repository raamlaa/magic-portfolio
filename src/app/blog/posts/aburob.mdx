## Behind the Scenes: Researching for Ahmad Aburob 🎬

In 2024, I joined **Ahmad Aburob’s content team** as a **YouTube content researcher and script strategist**. With over **9 million subscribers** and a channel known for bold storytelling and deep breakdowns, every video needed airtight research and fresh angles.

---

### 🧠 My Role

I contributed to:

- **Topic ideation**: analyzing trends and choosing angles with viral + cultural potential
- **Primary research**: sourcing facts from Arabic/English news, Reddit, books, and documentaries
- **Script support**: building strong story arcs and injecting emotional insight into data-heavy segments

### 📊 The Results

- **Combined views**: 10M+
- **Avg. watch time**: 7+ minutes
- **CTR**: consistently over 10%
- Strong community engagement and re-shares across TikTok and Instagram

---

### 🧩 Takeaway

Every topic was treated like a **mini-documentary**, with the goal of simplifying complex issues into sharp, emotional storytelling. I learned how to research fast, think strategically, and craft narratives that spark conversation — not just clicks.
